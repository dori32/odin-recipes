Shakshuka

Description

Enjoy this popular Middle Eastern egg dish at home: you'll love the blend of dried herbs and spices infused in the tomato sauce that is a base for eggs.

Ingredients

    2 tbsp olive oil 2 garlic 1 shallot 1 red chilli pepper 2 tsp smoked paprika 1/2 tsp oregano 1/2 tsp thyme 1 pinch caster sugar 675 gr tomatoes 4 eggs 150 gr cherry tomatoes 1 handful parsley 4 pita breads 2 tbsp butter 3 tsp seeds 3 tsp fennel seeds salt black pepper
