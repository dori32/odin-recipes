Chicken-Po-Boyy

Description

An easy recipe for chicken po boy, a delicious alternative version of the traditional Louisiana sandwich usually prepared with fried shrimps or roast beef.

Ingredients

  • 200 g, red and green, finely shredded cabbage
  • 1 medium, coarsely shredded carrot
  • 6g fresh, coarsley chopped mint
  • 6g fresh, coarsely chopped coriander
  • 1/2 tsp chili pepper
  • 1 1/2 tsp lime juice
  • 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • kosher salte, to taste
  • 110 g all purpose flour
  • 50 g yellow cornmeal
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 225 ml buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 675 g, chicken breasts, skinless, boneless, cut into 1 inch thick strips
  • vegetable oil for frying
  • 6 rools split bread
  • mayonnaise

    • Steps

    1. To prepare the chicken po boy first combine cabbage, carrot, mint, cilantro, and chile in a bowl.
    2. Stir in lime juice and olive oil and season with salt.
    3. In a pie plate mix flour, cornmeal, salt, and cayenne.
    4. In a medium bowl, whisk buttermilk and egg to combine.
    5. Dredge chicken in flour mixture, dip into buttermilk mixture, then return to flour mixture, turning to coat all sides.
    6. Pour 1 inch of oil into a large pan over medium-high heat.
    7. When oil reaches 175ยบ C, add chicken in a single layer, and cook until well browned all over and no longer pink in the center, about 6 minutes.
    8. As chicken is done, transfer to paper towels and sprinkle lightly with salt.
    9. Repeat process if necessary until all chicken is cooked.
    10. Spread sandwich rolls with mayonnaise.
    11. Divide chicken among rolls and top generously the chicken po boy with slaw.
